Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Tip Calculator app. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data with our minimalist, privacy-focused approach. We believe in transparency and protecting your privacy while providing accurate tip calculations.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal data to function:

  • Location Data (Optional): Only if you choose to enable region-specific tips. This is stored locally on your device.
  • User Preferences: Your preferred tip percentages and calculation settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identification information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing region-appropriate tip suggestions
  • Saving your calculation preferences
  • Improving the app's tip calculation accuracy

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage is used
  • Data is automatically cleared when you uninstall the app

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear all saved preferences through the app settings
  • Disable location-based features at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any privacy policy changes through app updates. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
