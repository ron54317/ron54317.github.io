Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Tip Calculator app. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data with our minimalist, privacy-focused approach. We believe in transparency and protecting your privacy while providing accurate tip calculations.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects and stores only essential data locally on your device:

  • App Settings:
    • Currency preferences
    • Theme settings
    • Haptic feedback settings
    • Location-based tips toggle state
  • Calculation Data:
    • Last used tip percentage
    • Most recent bill amount (temporary, only during calculation)
  • Region Information:
    • Selected region for tip recommendations (if location-based tips are enabled)
    • No actual GPS or location data is collected
  • What We DON'T Collect:
    • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
    • Payment information
    • Device location data
    • Usage statistics
    • Any data that leaves your device

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Remembering your app preferences for a better user experience
  • Providing appropriate tip suggestions based on your selected region
  • Maintaining your preferred settings between app launches
  • Enabling haptic feedback according to your preferences

All data processing happens locally on your device - no data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage is used
  • Data is automatically cleared when you uninstall the app

5. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear all saved preferences through the app settings
  • Disable location-based features at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data
